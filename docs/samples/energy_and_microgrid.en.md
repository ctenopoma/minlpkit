# Energy & Microgrid

Operation and design of power generation, energy storage, heat, and hydrogen supply/demand. A group of models including UC (Unit Commitment), battery dispatch, AC/DC power flow, and microgrid design, involving non-convexities such as quadratic fuel costs and power flows.

**16 models** (including 4 with particularly detailed business stories ⭐) / 9 models supporting the `scale` argument. The `scale` column ✓ means the scale is adjustable via `build_model(scale=...)`.
