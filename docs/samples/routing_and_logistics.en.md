# Routing and Logistics

Delivery, routing, vehicle assignment, and inventory transportation. A group of logistics models involving routing variables (discrete) and nonlinear costs.

**16 Models** / 8 models support the `scale` argument. A ✓ in the `scale` column means the scale can be varied with `build_model(scale=...)`.
